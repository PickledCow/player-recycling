Version: 1.0.6
Date: 14. 07. 2026
  Bugfixes:
    - Players no longer lose their logistic groups upon recycling.
  Balance Changes:
    - Quality characters mine even faster than before.
    - Quality characters are slightly faster than before.
    - Quality characters now have more weapon slots.
    - Quality characters can drop items farther away.
    - Quality characters can attack with their pickaxe from farther away.
    - Quality characters pickaxe does more damage per hit.
    - Quality characters start regenerating health quicker exiting combat.
    - Quality characters can ender vehicles from farther away.
    - Quality characters respawn faster after death. Quality is still lost on death.
  Info:
     - Changed how the crafting speed bonus was applied. It now is applied to the prototype instead of after changing character entities.
     - Vanilla quality player quality is being investigated and currently planning on how to integrate that system with this mod.
